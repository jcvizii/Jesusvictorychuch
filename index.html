History

Founded on November 1, 1991 at km. 6 Asin Road, Tadiangan, Tuba, Benguet under the name Love of Christ Fellowship. After some time, Ptr. Bisayan took over the church and named the church as Community Christian Fellowship.

After a few years, Ptr. Paciteng took over next and then Sis. Marilou Pi-ig and then came Bro. Abet Quilala.

Bro. Abet revamped the church building into a much bigger one and was named Community Christian Center. Few years passed, Bro. Abet turned over the leadership to Ptr. Jonathan Torres, a young pastor from Baguio Assembly Christian Church, Bonifacio Street, Baguio City.

Over the next years, the church was renamed to Jesus Christ our Gate of Life Church and currently Jesus Victory World Conquest Ministries Incorporation(Jesus Victory Church). The church now has a daughter church in San Luis-Extension and Tam-awan. As of 2020, the church has around 200 members in total.
